Moderation

As a moderator, you have the duty of maintain a safe and fun environment in the hotel. You may need these tool - use them whenever you need them, but don't abuse them.

Also - at all times you must comply with these rules.

  • Follow the regular hotel rules
  • Repect all members
  • Try to be nice to everyone and have patience

We expect you to follow these guidelines and good luck.
